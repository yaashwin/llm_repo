Organoid intelligence
(
OI
) is an emerging
field of study
in
computer science
and
biology
that develops and studies
biological wetware computing
using 3D cultures of human brain cells (or
brain organoids
) and
brain-machine interface
technologies.
[
1
]
Such technologies may be referred to as OIs.
Organoid intelligent computer systems can be an example of
biohybrid systems
.
